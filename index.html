Welcome to my creative universe — where art, design, and storytelling come together frame by frame. This portfolio is a collection of projects that reflect not only my skills, but my personality, curiosity, and passion for visual narratives. Every piece you see is original work created through collaboration, exploration, and a deep love for the craft.

Hello there, I'm Andrea...

I am a Graphic and Visual Designer with a Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design from Vietnam, and I am currently pursuing my diploma at Fanshawe College. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also gaining experience in the industry through an internship at General Access Inc. (April 2026).

With a passion for visual storytelling, I design with purpose and intent, reaching my audience and meeting the needs of my clients. With a solid background in teamwork, problem-solving, and customer service, I approach each project with a positive attitude, flexibility, and attention to detail.
